# Life Garden

The Life Garden is the central dashboard of the Tend application. It provides a holistic overview of all life areas, their health scores, active practices, and recent activity.

## Life Areas

Every user starts with 8 default life areas:

| Area | Description |
|------|------------|
| Health | Physical well-being, exercise, nutrition, sleep |
| Relationships | Family, friends, partnerships, social connections |
| Career | Professional growth, skills, purpose |
| Finances | Financial health, savings, investments |
| Mind | Mental health, learning, curiosity |
| Spirit | Inner peace, meaning, meditation |
| Creativity | Creative expression and imagination |
| Environment | Living space, nature, organization |

## Health Scores

Each life area has a health score (0-100) calculated from:
- **Weekly practice completion rate** (70% weight)
- **Active streak ratio** (20% weight)
- **Reflection recency** (10% weight)

## Practices

Practices are the daily/weekly/monthly actions that nurture each life area. They can be categorized as:
- **Habits** - Regular actions (exercise, reading)
- **Routines** - Structured sequences (morning routine)
- **Rituals** - Meaningful practices (meditation, gratitude)
- **Exercises** - Physical activities
- **Reflections** - Journaling and self-examination

## Reflections

Reflections are journal entries that can include:
- Free-form content
- Mood tracking (thriving, good, neutral, struggling, low)
- Gratitude items
- Insights and learnings
- Links to relevant life areas
